FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a project take?
Logos take 3–5 days, websites 1–3 weeks, and full brand packages 2–4 weeks. We always confirm timelines upfront before starting.
Do you quote in GYD or USD?
Both! We're flexible — GYD or USD, whichever works best for you and your business.
How many revisions do I get?
Unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied. We don't stop until you love the result.
Can you work with businesses outside Georgetown?
Absolutely — Berbice, Linden, Essequibo and beyond. Everything is handled online so location is never a constraint.
What files will I receive?
All standard formats — PNG, JPG, PDF, SVG, AI and more. You own all files outright with no restrictions.
